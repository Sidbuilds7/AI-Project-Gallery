Her story

Guidance rooted in care, not fear

Vinita began Celestial Pathway with a simple intention: to offer people a calm, safe place to be heard and gently guided. What started as quiet sessions for family and friends has grown into a practice trusted by clients across India and abroad since 2019.

Her approach is never about fear or pressure. Through Reiki, Numerology and Palmistry, she helps you understand what you’re feeling, see your situation more clearly, and take small, confident steps forward. Every session is personal, unhurried and completely confidential.
