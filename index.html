Good to know

New to this? Start here

A few honest answers for first-timers — so booking feels easy, not intimidating.

I’ve never done this before. Where do I start?

Right here. Just send a WhatsApp message saying how you’ve been feeling — you don’t need the “right words” or any prior experience. Vinita will help you choose the session that fits, with zero pressure.

Are sessions available online?

Yes. Most sessions are held online, so you can book from anywhere in India or abroad. This makes it easy for NRIs and clients in different time zones.

How do I book and pay?

Everything starts on WhatsApp. Message us, we’ll suggest a session and a time, and share the simple booking and payment details from there.

What if I’m not sure which service I need?

That’s completely normal. Tell us what you’re going through and we’ll gently recommend whether Reiki, Numerology or Palmistry is the best starting point.

Is everything private and confidential?

Always. Your session is a safe, judgment-free space, and anything you share stays strictly between you and Vinita.

Which languages are sessions held in?

Sessions can be held in Hindi and English. If you have a preference, just mention it when you message us.
