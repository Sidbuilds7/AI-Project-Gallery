Who it’s for

Anyone curious to understand themselves more deeply, or seeking gentle guidance on relationships, emotions and direction.

If you’re struggling with…

  • Confusion or tension in relationships

A calm, conversational reading focused on understanding rather than prediction — helping you align with what feels true and choose your next step with confidence.
